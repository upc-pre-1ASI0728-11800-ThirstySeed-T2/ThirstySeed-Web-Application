What happens after creating a farm?

A farm works as the main container for your agricultural operation. After registering it, you will be able to:

  • 🌱
    Create plots

    Define areas and sub-zones for inventory.

  • 📡
    Assign IoT nodes

    Connect sensor devices to collect data.

  • 📊
    Start monitoring

    View moisture, temp and alerts in real time.
