What happens after creating a zone?

A water zone works as the main container for regional water management. After registering it, you will be able to:

  • 🌾
    Link farms

    Associate agricultural farms that consume water from this zone.

  • 📊
    Monitor consumption

    View total water consumed across all linked farms and plots.

  • ⚠️
    Detect critical areas

    Identify zones with high demand and coordinate water resources.
